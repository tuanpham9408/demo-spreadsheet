# Syncfusion License Configuration

## Getting Your License Key

1. Visit https://www.syncfusion.com/sales/products
2. Sign up for a free trial or purchase a license
3. Go to your Syncfusion account dashboard
4. Navigate to "License & Downloads"
5. Copy your license key

## Registering Your License

Open `src/App.tsx` and find this line (around line 8):

```typescript
// registerLicense('YOUR_LICENSE_KEY');
```

Uncomment it and replace with your actual key:

```typescript
registerLicense('YOUR_ACTUAL_LICENSE_KEY_HERE');
```

## License Types

- **Community License**: Free for companies < $1M revenue
- **Trial License**: 30-day free trial
- **Commercial License**: For larger organizations

## Without License

The app will work but show a license banner. All features remain functional during the trial period.

For more information: https://help.syncfusion.com/common/essential-studio/licensing/overview
